# Proposal: Hotel Booking Communication Flow
## Santorini Breeze Hotel — AI Automation Engagement

---

## Problem We're Solving

Your front desk team currently handles every guest communication by hand — copying booking details from Cloudbeds into email templates, remembering to send pre-arrival information three days before check-in, and building invoices manually in Excel. In peak season, this takes three people a significant portion of their day. Three to four times per week in August, guests arrive without having received directions, forcing emergency calls that interrupt your team when they're already at maximum load. Invoice errors — wrong dates, wrong VAT — have caused disputes with corporate clients. This is costing your business approximately **€5,900 per year in staff time alone**, before counting guest experience damage and the stress on your team.

---

## What We're Building

An end-to-end communication automation that runs silently in the background and handles every routine guest touchpoint — with zero manual steps from your team.

**Here's exactly what it does:**

1. The moment a booking is confirmed in Cloudbeds (from Booking.com, Airbnb, or your website), a **confirmation email** is sent automatically to your guest — personalized with their name, room details, dates, and your cancellation policy.

2. Three days before every check-in, at 9:00 in the morning, a **pre-arrival email** is sent automatically — with directions, parking info, check-in time, WiFi password, and breakfast details. No one has to remember. No guest arrives without it.

3. On checkout day, a correctly calculated **PDF invoice** is generated and emailed to the guest — with proper VAT (13%), the right dates and rates, and your hotel logo. For corporate clients, it is produced in both English and Greek.

4. When a potential guest fills in the contact form on your website, they receive an **automatic acknowledgement** within seconds, and Maria gets a formatted summary in her inbox ready to reply to.

---

## What It Doesn't Do

- Replace human judgment for unusual situations, disputes, or special requests
- Connect to the Greek tax authority system (myDATA) — this can be a follow-on project
- Manage Airbnb in-app messaging (Airbnb's platform does not allow external automation of their messaging system)
- Handle cancellations or rebooking — this is a separate flow for a later phase

---

## What Success Looks Like

At the end of the first peak season after launch:
- Maria sends **zero** manual confirmation or pre-arrival emails
- **Zero** guests arrive without pre-arrival information
- Invoice errors are eliminated — every invoice is correct on the first send
- Your team's time in peak season shifts from routine typing to actual hospitality
